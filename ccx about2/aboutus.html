About ConferenceConneX

connecting this new world.

ConferenceConneX is a non-profit organization dedicated to making e-conferences more accessible and helping to create a direct connection between students and organizations. We have established this platform to serve as a “one-stop-shop” for students to be able to register for any conference that matches their interests, and also for organizations to promote their conferences to a wider audience.

OUR Services

Connection Match

ConferenceConneX aims to address the networking gap in teleconferencing though a matching algorithm that connects people based on similar research/expertise interests and openness to varying degrees of collaboration (ex: idea exchanges to research projects to mentorship and more). The pool of potential matches and their relevant information will be voluntarily collected from each conference’s registration and the matching algorithm will be weighted accounting for equity, fairness, and compatibility of choices.

Conference Hub

This platform will be integrated with online conferences’ registration, where it recruits delegates interested in sharing their interests and collaborating on projects. The online database of those interested/available will be accessible and visible only to those sharing similar interests and project expectations. This allows delegates to reach out to the individuals on their customized list during and after the conference in order to foster meaningful connections and research collaborations

Promote Your Conference

For each project we establish relationships with partners who we know will help us create added value for your project. As well as bringing together the public and private sectors, we make sector-overarching links to gather knowledge and to learn from each other. The way we undertake projects is based on permanently applying.
